# Skill Dataset (10 Cases)

## Overview
This dataset contains 10 examples of transforming raw, human-written prompts into structured, AI-optimized prompts using foundational prompting techniques.

Each case follows:
Input → Refined Prompt Output

---

## Case 1 — Content Writing (LinkedIn)

### Input
i want to post something about ai agents like how they help business and make it sound professional but not too complex also maybe make people interested and not too long

### Output
Role: Senior marketing strategist specializing in AI-driven business growth  
Task: Write a LinkedIn post about how AI agents improve business productivity  
Context: Audience is mid-level professionals unfamiliar with AI beyond chatbots; goal is to educate and build authority  
Constraints: Max 150 words, simple language, professional but approachable tone, no jargon  
Structure: Hook → 2–3 short paragraphs → closing CTA  
Think First: Consider clarity and engagement for non-technical readers
